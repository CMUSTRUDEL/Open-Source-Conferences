Another awesome online event with some Healthcare Technology leaders. We will be hearing about what its like to be a technology leader in a HealthTech environment, which couldn't come at a better time, considering the whole industry is under a real spotlight.

First we'll be hearing from Stephen Sitton who is the Senior Manager of Engineering for Medidata Solutions. His talk will be "Building a Tech Culture at Medidata".

Then we'll be hearing from Venkat Balakumar who has a strong profile as an interim CTO for healthcare start-ups, and he will be discussing: "Transformation in the new Normal".

The event is sponsored by Pusher and Medidata Solutions.
