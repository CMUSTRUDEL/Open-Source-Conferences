Publication date: 2020-05-07

Description: 
	Livecoded Compositions is a series of musical motifs and remixes using 
livecoding and an analog synthesizer. The compositions are created with 
the open-source tool TidalCycles and a trusty Korg MS-20 synthesizer.

Dan Gorelick is a Brooklyn based musician and technologist whose work 
focuses on creating musical experiences that are collaborative and 
accessible. His work combines hardware and web technologies to create 
sound installations and musical performances. He is a classically 
trained cellist and lifelong musician. His creative practice explores 
the playful side of technology, creating web experiments and musical 
instruments with code. As a member of the Livecode.NYC community he 
teaches Livecoding workshops and performs music at Livecoding shows.
