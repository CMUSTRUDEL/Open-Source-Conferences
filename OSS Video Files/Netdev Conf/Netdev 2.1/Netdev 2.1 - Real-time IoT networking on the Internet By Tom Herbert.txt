Title: Netdev 2.1 - Real-time IoT networking on the Internet By Tom Herbert
Publication date: 2017-05-02
Playlist: Netdev 2.1
Description: 
	In this talk presented to Netdev 2.1 in Montreal on the 8th of Apr/2017, Tom Herbert  presents the motivation and direction to support the emerging class of IoT applications and protocols that require latencies measured in milliseconds on the Internet. He argues that these requirements are needed for "life-critical applications".
