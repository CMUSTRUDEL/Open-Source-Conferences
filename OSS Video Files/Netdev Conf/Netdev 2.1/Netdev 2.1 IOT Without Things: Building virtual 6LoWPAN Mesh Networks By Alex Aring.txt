Title: Netdev 2.1 IOT Without Things: Building virtual 6LoWPAN Mesh Networks By Alex Aring
Publication date: 2017-06-03
Playlist: Netdev 2.1
Description: 
	In this talk given on the 8th of April, 2017 at Netdev 2.1 in Montreal Alex Aring talks about how to build a virtual IOT environment for testing purposes to overcome cost and complexity of building a real one.
Alex describes his approach to use the virtual IEEE 802.15.4 driver "fakelb" to emulate multiple virtual PHYs which can talk to each others.
