Title: Netdev 0x13 - Devlink health reporting and recovery system
Publication date: 2019-05-03
Playlist: Netdev 0x13 - Day 2 - Track 1 + 2 - Mar 21 2019
Description: 
	Eran Ben Elisha discusses the design behind the Devlink health mechanism. 

The Devlink health mechanism provides Real Time Alerting when something bad happens to a PCI device. The primary goals are to provide alert debug information, self healing of discovered issues and help collect useful debug details for vendor support.

What should a Device driver author creating a "health reporter" provide to the Devlink health subsystem? Tune in to hear it all.
