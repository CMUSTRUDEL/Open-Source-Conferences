Title: Alessio Siniscalchi - Citizen Science: Tracking Aliens with Python!
Publication date: 2015-08-08
Playlist: EuroPython 2015
Description: 
	Alessio Siniscalchi - Citizen Science: Tracking Aliens with Python!
[EuroPython 2015]
[21 July 2015]
[Bilbao, Euskadi, Spain]

The talk discusses the challenges of implementing a Citizen Science
Paradigm in a Python-centric platform, and the solutions devised for
the System for observation and monitoring of Marine Alien Species,
currently used by the italian Institute for Environmental Protection
and Research (ISPRA). "Alien" Species means species introduced into a
natural environment where they are not normally found.

Topics includes strategies for crowd-friendly forms, work-flow
definition for collected data, choice of the best technologies for its
components: app for android devices, web application for citizens and
experts, webGIS for data browsing and web services for data exporting.
