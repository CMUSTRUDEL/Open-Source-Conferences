Title: Leadership for Developers - Hampton Catlin
Publication date: 2020-12-17
Playlist: All Things Open 2020 - Front-End Engineering Track
Description: 
	Presented by: Hampton Catlin, Veue
Presented at All Things Open 2020 - Front-End Engineering Track

Abstract: There is often a lot of tension in the field of programming around what it means to progress in your career, whether by seniority as an individual contributor or trying your hand at people leadership. We'll discuss the differences of types of leadership, how to understand your own leadership style and potential, and give a framework learned over 15 years in leadership in how to encourage leadership ethics in both hands-on and people-management roles.
