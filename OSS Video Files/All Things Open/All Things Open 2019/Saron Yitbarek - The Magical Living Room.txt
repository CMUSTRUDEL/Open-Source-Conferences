Title: Saron Yitbarek - The Magical Living Room
Publication date: 2019-11-04
Playlist: All Things Open 2019
Description: 
	It’s easier than ever to learn to code. Everyone – from engineers to entrepreneurs – has access now more than ever to free and and cheap books, courses, websites to level up; no matter who you are or where you come from, you too, can learn to code. But learning to code and becoming a developer isn’t simply about the existence of resources- it’s about the community that comes with it.

In her talk,Saron explores what it takes to build a truly inclusive and accessible developer community from lessons she learned from her mom growing up.
