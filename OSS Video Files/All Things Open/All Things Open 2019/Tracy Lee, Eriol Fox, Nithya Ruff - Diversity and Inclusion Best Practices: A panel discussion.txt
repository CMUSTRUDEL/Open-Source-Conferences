Title: Tracy Lee, Eriol Fox, Nithya Ruff - Diversity and Inclusion Best Practices: A panel discussion
Publication date: 2019-11-06
Playlist: All Things Open 2019
Description: 
	Diversity and Inclusion are hot topics in the technology world today, and justifiably so. Everyone agrees more needs to be done, but what does that actually look like? Attend this session to hear an honest discussion around best practices and lessons learned, from panel members that are true thought leaders in the space. Attendees will leave with proven action items and ideas that are tried-and-true, as well as a few actions to avoid. Everyone will leave more informed and in a better position to implement plans and programs.
