Title: Nikema Prophet – The Barriers to Entry I Face as a Mom in Tech
Publication date: 2019-11-02
Playlist: All Things Open 2019
Description: 
	How do you count the people who couldn’t be in the room because they’ve been shut out? In this session you’ll learn the structural barriers low-income parents face as they attempt to break into tech. We will cover the awareness gaps that lead to the conditions that effectively shut out a population that has much to contribute.
