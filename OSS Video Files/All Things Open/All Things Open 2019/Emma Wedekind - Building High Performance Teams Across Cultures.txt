Title: Emma Wedekind - Building High Performance Teams Across Cultures
Publication date: 2019-11-06
Playlist: All Things Open 2019
Description: 
	Have you ever worked with team members from other parts of the world? If so, have you ever experienced misunderstandings or had trouble communicating? That’s because each culture communicates differently and by learning to recognize these differences we can enhance our collaboration and build high-performing teams. In this talk we’ll delve into methods for collaborating across cultures and giving and receiving feedback so you can enhance your team cadence.
