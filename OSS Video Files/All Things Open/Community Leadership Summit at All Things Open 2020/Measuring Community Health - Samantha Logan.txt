Title: Measuring Community Health - Samantha Logan
Publication date: 2020-12-03
Playlist: Community Leadership Summit at All Things Open 2020
Description: 
	Presented by: Samantha Logan, SociallyConstructed.Online
Presented at All Things Open 2020, Community Leadership Summit

Abstract: Communities are notoriously difficult to measure because they combine tangible and intangible value. Possibly the most critical question though is, "Is our community healthy?" In this session, we want to dig into what "healthy" means and what we need to measure to truly understand community health.
