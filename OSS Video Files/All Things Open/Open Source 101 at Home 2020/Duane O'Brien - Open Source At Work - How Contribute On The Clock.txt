Description: 
	Open Source At Work - How Contribute On The Clock

Presented at: Open Source 101 at Home 2020
Presented by: Duane O'Brien, Indeed

Abstract: You've use open source. You love it. You've always wanted to give back, but you haven't been sure where to start. Wouldn't it be great if there was some way you could contribute to open source as part of your job?

This talk will give you all the tools you need to get up an running as an open source contributor as part of your day job. Attendees will learn:

 - Tools to help secure approval from your employer
 - How to get buy-in from your manager
 - How to identify projects and contribution opportunities
 - Techniques for accounting for your open source work during performance reviews
