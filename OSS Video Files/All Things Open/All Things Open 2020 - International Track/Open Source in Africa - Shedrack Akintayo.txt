Title: Open Source in Africa - Shedrack Akintayo
Publication date: 2020-12-18
Playlist: All Things Open 2020 - International Track
Description: 
	Presented by: Shedrack Akintayo, Cloud Foundry Foundation
Presented at All Things Open 2020 - International Track

Abstract: According to Github reports earlier this year, A certain number of African countries have been listed amount top contributors to open source and also users of Github. Countries like, Nigeria, Morocco, Ghana were all listed. In this talk, I'll talk about how open source is shaping the future of software development in Africa, very important communities supporting open source in Africa, and key players supporting open source in Africa. I'll also suggest better ways companies in more developed countries around the globe can support Open Source development and Software in Africa.
