Title: Learning to Maximize Product Impact Through Open Source - Marissa Crosby
Publication date: 2020-12-16

Description: 
	Presented by: Marissa Crosby, Optum
Presented at All Things Open 2020 - 101 Track

Abstract: Product development is often about solving your organization’s most painful problems, but what if these problems are just as prevalent beyond your enterprise? Is there value in delivering a great product internally and externally? The answer is yes! Learn how to elevate your products and teams by harnessing the power of open source software – from design to operations, and everything in-between.
