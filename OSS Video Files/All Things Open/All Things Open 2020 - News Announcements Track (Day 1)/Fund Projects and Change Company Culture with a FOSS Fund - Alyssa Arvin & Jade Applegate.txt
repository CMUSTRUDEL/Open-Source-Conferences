Title: Fund Projects and Change Company Culture with a FOSS Fund - Alyssa Arvin & Jade Applegate
Publication date: 2020-12-09
Playlist: All Things Open 2020 - News Announcements Track (Day 1)
Description: 
	Presented by: Alyssa Arvin, Salesforce & Jade Applegate, Indeed
Presented at All Things Open 2020 - Community Track

Abstract: In this talk, we will introduce the FOSS Contributor Fund framework and the importance of supporting projects as a model for open source sustainability. Attendees will gain an understanding of what a FOSS Fund is, why they should consider implementing it at their company, and how to go about doing so.
