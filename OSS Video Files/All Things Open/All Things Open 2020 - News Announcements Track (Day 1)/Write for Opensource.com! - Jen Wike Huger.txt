	Presented by: Jen Wike Huger, Red Hat
Presented at All Things Open 2020 - News/Announcements Track (Day 1)

Abstract: Share your open source experience with a large audience of 2 million monthly readers on Opensource.com. Work with a professional team of editors to craft and revise your idea or draft for publication. Becoming a writer for Opensource.com makes you a part of our community of open source enthusiasts ranging from beginners to seasoned practitioners. In this session, learn about popular writing topics and how to submit your article and get it published!
