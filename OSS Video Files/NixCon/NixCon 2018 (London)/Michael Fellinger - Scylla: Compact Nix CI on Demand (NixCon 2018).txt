Title: Michael Fellinger - Scylla: Compact Nix CI on Demand (NixCon 2018)
Publication date: 2018-11-05
Playlist: NixCon 2018 (London)
Description: 
	Using some of the features of Nix 2, it's possible to build a rather fully
featured but easy to use and understand Continues Integration Service.

Scylla is an attempt to create a new platform for CI, more similar to ofBorg,
but specialized for many smaller projects.
