Title: Vienna.js, Introduction to Smart Assistants  - Alexa vs Google Home, February 2019
Publication date: 2019-03-06
Playlist: viennaJS
Description: 
	Talk By Jakob Reiter:

Smart Assistants (most common Alexa and Google Home) are more and more pushing into our living rooms. In this talk I will present the two major platform capabilities, compare their up and downs from a developer perspective and show some nodejs code on what developers can do with them.
