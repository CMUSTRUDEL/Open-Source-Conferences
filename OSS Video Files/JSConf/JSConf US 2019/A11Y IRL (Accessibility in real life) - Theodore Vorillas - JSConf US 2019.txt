Title: A11Y IRL (Accessibility in real life) - Theodore Vorillas - JSConf US 2019
Publication date: 2019-09-11
Playlist: JSConf US 2019
Description: 
	The year is 2019, and accessibility is not a buzzword anymore. It's now imperative that information is readily available to everyone, regardless of their disability or financial status.

In this talk, you'll find out how to create accessible real-life objects, using run-of-the-mill JavaScript and the basic principles of Internet of Things.
