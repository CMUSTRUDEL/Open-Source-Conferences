Title: From Idea to IPO: Convincing Your CTO about GraphQL (Abhi Arora)
Publication date: 2018-12-05
Playlist: GraphQL Summit 2018
Description: 
	Talk from GraphQL Summit 2018 - Nov 7, 2018

Abhi Arora is the CTO and founder of Hyrecar, a car-sharing for ride-sharing startup that went public in June 2018. "You are super excited about GraphQL, are using it in personal projects, and want your entire team to adopt it!" This talk will cover HyreCar's journey from learning about GraphQL to adopting it for our entire business.
