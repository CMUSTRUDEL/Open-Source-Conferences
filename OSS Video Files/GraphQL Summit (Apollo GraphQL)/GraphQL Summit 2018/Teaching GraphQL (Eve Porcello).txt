Title: Teaching GraphQL (Eve Porcello)
Publication date: 2018-12-05
Playlist: GraphQL Summit 2018
Description: 
	Talk from GraphQL Summit 2018 - Nov 7, 2018

Eve Porcello, Engineer and Instructor at Moon Highway, has taught numerous courses for LinkedIn Learning and is the co-author of O’Reilly’s Learning GraphQL and Learning React. Even if your bio bills you as an engineer, developer, or manager, she has a secret for you: you are also a teacher. Whether you’re ramping up a new hire, mentoring a co-worker, writing documentation, or giving a technical talk, you’re constantly expected to share knowledge as requirements and technologies rapidly change.
