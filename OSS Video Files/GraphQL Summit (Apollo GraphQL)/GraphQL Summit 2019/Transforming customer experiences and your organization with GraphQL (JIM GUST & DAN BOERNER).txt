Title: Transforming customer experiences and your organization with GraphQL (JIM GUST & DAN BOERNER)
Publication date: 2019-11-02
Playlist: GraphQL Summit 2019
Description: 
	Dan Boerner and Jim Gust share the expected and unexpected ways that GraphQL is transforming how Expedia Group creates seamless customer experiences across their front-end platforms. They share hard fought lessons and mistakes during their journey selling the promise of GraphQL, keeping momentum building beyond the initial POC team, and scaling a graph that spans many services and teams.
