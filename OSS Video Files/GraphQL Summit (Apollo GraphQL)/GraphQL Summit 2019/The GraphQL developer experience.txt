Title: The GraphQL developer experience
Publication date: 2019-11-02
Playlist: GraphQL Summit 2019
Description: 
	With GraphQL, it’s easier than ever to set ourselves up to have a great developer experience that brings more productivity and satisfaction to our jobs. Danielle Man, Engineering Manager at Apollo, talks about how the shift from legacy APIs to GraphQL can lead to happier, more productive development.

Watch Danielle walk through a live demo of connecting backend services to UI with a seamless programming model that extends from server to client using Apollo GraphQL and React.

You’ll see what the modern app developer experience looks like, learn how to get started, and discover what you may not even realize you’re missing.
