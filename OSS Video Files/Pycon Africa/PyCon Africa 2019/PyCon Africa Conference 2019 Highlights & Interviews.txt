Title: PyCon Africa Conference 2019 Highlights & Interviews
Publication date: 2019-09-02
Playlist: PyCon Africa 2019
Description: 
	PyCon Africa 2019 was the first-ever pan-African meeting of the Python programming community, and took place in Accra, Ghana from the 6th - 10th August. It included five days of workshops, talks, sprints and events such as a Django Girls tutorial.

Here are some of the highlights and interviews from PyCon Africa 2019. For a more detailed look into the event read our official conference report: https://africa.pycon.org/report-2019/
