Title: AfroPython: Empowering black people using Python in Brazil - Felipe De Morais
Publication date: 2019-11-18
Playlist: PyCon Africa 2019
Description: 
	In Felipe's talk we get to know that most of the people in Brazil are black but this people are sub-represented in Tech. It's also the second country with more black people in the world, just behind Nigeria. This talk is to showcase an initiative created in Brazil to include and empower black people through technology called AfroPython, filling in this gap.
