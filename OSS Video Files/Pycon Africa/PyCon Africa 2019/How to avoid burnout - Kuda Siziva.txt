Title: How to avoid burnout - Kuda Siziva
Publication date: 2019-11-18
Playlist: PyCon Africa 2019
Description: 
	Frequently our communities are affected by burnout and we do not discuss it regardless of its impact. In Kuda's presentation he aims at addressing burnout, what it is, what are its effects on the growth of python communities and how to recover from one and help each other grow the python community.
