Title: A Reintroduction to Data Science - Aseda Owusua Addai-Deseh
Publication date: 2019-11-23
Playlist: PyCon Africa 2019
Description: 
	There are many talks and workshops on Data Science, but this particular talk would reintroduce Data Science to its audience in a beginner-friendly and pragmatic way. Aseda's talk is intended to whet the audience's appetite to become Data Scientists or employ Data Scientists in order to move their businesses to the next level.
