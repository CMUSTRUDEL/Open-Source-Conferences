Title: Bridging the talent gap between Python communities and industry - Kelvin Oyanna
Publication date: 2019-11-23
Playlist: PyCon Africa 2019
Description: 
	One of the biggest challenge for most companies today is finding the right talents. Tech communities on the other hand are gradually becoming the incubation ground for developing tech talents. Kelvin's talk seeks to address the disconnection between both worlds & suggest ways to bridge the talent gap.
