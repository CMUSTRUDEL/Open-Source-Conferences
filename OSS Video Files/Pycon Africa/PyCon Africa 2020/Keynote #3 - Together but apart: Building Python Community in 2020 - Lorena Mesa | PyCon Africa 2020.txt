Description: 
	Our 3rd Keynote at PyCon Africa 2020 was delivered by Lorena Mesa titled: Together but apart: Building Python Community in 2020. 

Political scientist turned coder, Lorena Mesa is a GitHub data engineer,  Python Software Foundation Chair, JOSS editor, & PyLadies Chicago co-organizer. Lorena's time at Obama for America & background in research and applied mathematics drove Lorena to pursue a career in engineering and data science. One part activist, one part Star Wars fanatic, and another part Trekkie, Lorena abides by the motto to "live long and prosper".
