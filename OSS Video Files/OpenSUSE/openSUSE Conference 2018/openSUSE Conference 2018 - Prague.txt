Description: 
	openSUSE Conference 2018 May 25 - 27, 2018

Prague, Czech Republic

The openSUSE Conference begins Friday, May 25, and will finish on Sunday, May 27. The openSUSE Conference is the annual openSUSE community event that brings people from around the world together to meet and collaborate. The organized talks, workshops, and BoF sessions provide a framework around more casual meet ups and hack sessions. A party here and there provides the time to relax and have fun, making connections on a more personal level.
