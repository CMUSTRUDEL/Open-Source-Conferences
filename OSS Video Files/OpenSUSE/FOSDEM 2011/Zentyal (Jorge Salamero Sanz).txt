Title: Zentyal (Jorge Salamero Sanz)
Publication date: 2011-02-10
Playlist: FOSDEM 2011
Description: 
	Zentyal (formerly eBox Platform) is a Linux small business server, that can act as Gateway, Infrastructure Manager, Unified Threat Manager, Office Server, Unified Communication Server or a combination of them. These functionalities are tightly integrated, automating most tasks, avoiding mistakes and saving time for system administrators.Zentyal is based on Ubuntu server, being able to manage services in a computer network like advanced routing, firewall and traffic shaping, DHCP, DNS, RADIUS, VPN, proxy, IDS, mail, file and printer sharing, VoIP, IM and more.
