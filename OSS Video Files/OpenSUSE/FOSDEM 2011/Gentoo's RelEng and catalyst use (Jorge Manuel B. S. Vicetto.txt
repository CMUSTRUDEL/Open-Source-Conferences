Title: Gentoo's RelEng and catalyst use (Jorge Manuel B. S. Vicetto
Publication date: 2011-02-05
Playlist: FOSDEM 2011
Description: 
	A short introduction to Gentoo's Release Engineering[1] team and its current model to produce media to install Gentoo.An introduction to the the catalyst[2][3] tool and a description of the current configuration to produce our official stages and isos, specifically the weekly autobuilds.A short demonstration of how to configure and use catalyst to create stages and isos on one's hardware.
