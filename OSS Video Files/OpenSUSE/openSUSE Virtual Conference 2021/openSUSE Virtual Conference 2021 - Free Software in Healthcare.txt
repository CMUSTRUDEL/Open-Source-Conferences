Title: openSUSE Virtual Conference 2021 - Free Software in Healthcare
Publication date: 2021-06-19
Playlist: openSUSE Virtual Conference 2021
Description: 
	https://media.ccc.de/v/3513-free-software-in-healthcare



Health is a very special 'good', and it deserves special attention in terms of security and privacy.
But what is the reality?
This talk gives an overview about the status of free/libre systems in healthcare, the difference in development & procurement compared to proprietary solutions - and how openSUSE supports healthcare software 'the safe way'
