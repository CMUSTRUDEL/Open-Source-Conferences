Title: Business Challenges and Opportunities with Gutenberg
Publication date: 2019-04-13
Playlist: WordCamp Ahmedabad 2018
Description: 
	Gutenberg is the upcoming new content editor for WordPress 5.0. It is going to change the way we publish and develop content in WordPress.
Also, it has generated a lot of controversies. During the talk, Aslam will be discussing different challenges and opportunities that Gutenberg brings in as it merges to the WordPress core.
