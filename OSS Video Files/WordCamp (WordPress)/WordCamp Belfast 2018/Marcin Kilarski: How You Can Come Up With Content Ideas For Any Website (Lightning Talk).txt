Title: Marcin Kilarski: How You Can Come Up With Content Ideas For Any Website (Lightning Talk)
Publication date: 2019-05-08
Playlist: WordCamp Belfast 2018
Description: 
	Do you ask yourself “what kind of content I should write on my or my client’s site to attract more customers”? If yes, this presentation will show you a few ways of using the date to find relevant topics in any industry that these users are looking for.
I will try to help you replace a feeling of uncertainty that you might have when you are deciding what pages or articles you need to write next.
Instead, you will be confident that a large group of readers or potential customers are looking for them.
