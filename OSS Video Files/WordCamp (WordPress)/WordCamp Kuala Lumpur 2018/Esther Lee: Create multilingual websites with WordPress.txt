Title: Esther Lee: Create multilingual websites with WordPress
Publication date: 2019-05-13
Playlist: WordCamp Kuala Lumpur 2018
Description: 
	One of the high demand services in WordPress development is creating multilingual websites. If your target customers are from different country with different language, it is important for your website or your online WooComerce store to have multiple language and be translated, instead of having different website for different country. I will explain how to create a professional multilingual websites with WordPress.
