Description: 
	Do you ever have the feeling to have little time for yourself? Or that you never have the chance to do the things that you love? And what about being always late, to run all day, but never be able to complete the tasks on your to-do-list?
You’re not alone! We all went through this and certainly some time management techniques can help you to be more productive (I’ll tell you some in this talk!), but there is a secret ingredient that you have to add in your day to really lead it with satisfaction. Curious to know more about it?
