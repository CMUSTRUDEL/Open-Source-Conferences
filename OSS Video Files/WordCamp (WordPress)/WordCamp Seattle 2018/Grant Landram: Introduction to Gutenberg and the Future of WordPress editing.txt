Title: Grant Landram: Introduction to Gutenberg and the Future of WordPress editing
Publication date: 2020-04-09
Playlist: WordCamp Seattle 2018
Description: 
	Grant will walk you through an introduction to Gutenberg – the new editing interface coming in WordPress 5.0 – covering the short-term tactical changes you can expect in WordPress 5.0 later this year, as well as a conceptual exploration of what it means for the future of site building and publishing with WordPress.
