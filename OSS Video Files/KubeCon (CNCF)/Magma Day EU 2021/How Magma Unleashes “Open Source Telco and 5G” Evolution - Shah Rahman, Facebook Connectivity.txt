How Magma Unleashes “Open Source Telco and 5G” Evolution - Shah Rahman, Facebook Connectivity

Magma is one of the most exciting open-source projects that is enabling the telco industry build products and solutions based on open and disaggregated software stacks. It is also empowering the ecosystem comprising startups, vendors, system integrators and mobile operators build those solutions for a wide range of use cases that are possible with 5G. See through how Magma evolves as a key component of the future networks that look much like the hyperscalers than the traditional telco networks.
