Title: Steve Ovens - Getting Kids involved with computing
Publication date: 2019-06-18
Playlist: 2019 SouthEast LinuxFest
Description: 
	SouthEast Linux Fest 2019
Have you ever wondered how to engage children with technology? Do you want to guide them down the path to open source? It can be hard to search for specific

Kids don't often care about the higher level implications of things. They just want to play games and have fun. In this talk we'll cover various age groups and what some tools you might employ to get kids used to linux and open source. This talk is from the perspective of parents who home school 2 boys (7 and 3).  I will talk about some of the programs and techniques that have sparked interest
