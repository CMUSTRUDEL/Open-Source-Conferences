Title: Susan Sons - Securing the Weird
Publication date: 2019-06-19
Playlist: 2019 SouthEast LinuxFest
Description: 
	SouthEast Linux Fest 2019
Information security is usually focused on web servers and email, laptops and mobile devices.  What happens when the things that need securing are volcano-top telescopes, oceanic drones, research vessels, and Antarctic sensor nets?  Veteran information security professional Susan Sons describes working through the security needs of strange and challenging science and R&D technologies based on first principles.
