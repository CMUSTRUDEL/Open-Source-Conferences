Title: Mark R. White - Running With Giants: How To Sell Open Source To Your Company
Publication date: 2019-06-18
Playlist: 2019 SouthEast LinuxFest
Description: 
	SouthEast Linux Fest 2019
As technical professionals, our acumen for making our ideas about technology actionable are, stereotypically, somewhat lacking. My talk is about how to take tried and true sales methodologies and put them to use in convincing our respective organizations to implement these technologies. This talk generally comes across as good sales advice with a large helping of self-help material. I call the presentation Running with Giants because it's specifically geared at taking on the big corporations such as Microsoft and IBM.
