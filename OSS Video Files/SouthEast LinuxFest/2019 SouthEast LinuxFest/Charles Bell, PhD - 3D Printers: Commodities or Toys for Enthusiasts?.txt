Title: Charles Bell, PhD - 3D Printers: Commodities or Toys for Enthusiasts?
Publication date: 2019-06-18
Playlist: 2019 SouthEast LinuxFest
Description: 
	SouthEast Linux Fest 2019
It has been over a decade since affordable 3D printers have become widely available. Many expected a massive revolution in manufacturing and others predicted 3D printers would populate nearly every home. Has any of these expectations been met? Have the predictions come true?

Join us as we discover the path 3D printing has taken of the recent years including the state of 3D printers today. If you've ever wanted to know what all of the fuss was about, drop by and see how you can learn what 3D printing can do for you.
