	Lightning Talk: How Zowe and Open Source Made Me Talk to the Mainframe (literally) - Yongkook (Alex) Kim, Vicom Infinity

We are living in a world of many smart technologies. Almost 1 out of every 4 grown up in US own smart speakers such as Alexa and Google Assistant. While it's fascinating to use it for personal questions, what other ways can we use voice technology at enterprise work space? Started with Zowe, Yongkook shares how Vicom Infinity Voice Assistant (VIVA) has been using open source efforts to enable connecting with the mainframe, securely.
