Title: Pathao: Navigating with OSM in Bangladesh while Community blends in with Corporate
Publication date: 2018-08-20
Playlist: SotM 2018, Day 2, S.0.2
Description: 
	Tasauf A Baki Billah (OSMBD, BHOOT, Pathao), State of the Map 2018
https://2018.stateofthemap.org/2018/T018-Pathao__Navigating__with_OSM_in_Bangladesh_while_Community_blends_in_with_Corporate/

Will focus on recent collaboration of the BHOOT's from OpenStreetMap Bangladesh community with Pathao, the most popular ride sharing service provider & technology based startup; How the local community & the global OpenStreetMap platform getting benefited through the collaboration; The Mammoth level of contribution done by the team and how far they have taken the data size of the region in this small period of time and how they are pushing the community to grow in a more coordinated way.
