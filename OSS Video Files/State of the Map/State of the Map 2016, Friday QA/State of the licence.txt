Title: State of the licence
Publication date: 2016-10-14
Playlist: State of the Map 2016, Friday QA
Description: 
	Simon Poole at State of the Map 2016
http://2016.stateofthemap.org/2016/state-of-the-licence/

In September 2016 it will 4 years will have passed since OpenStreetMap changed its licence from CC by-SA 2.0 to the ODbL 1.0. Time to make a tally of the aftermath.

The session will cover * a final damage estimate * the guideline process, guidelines in force and what is in the works * the day-to-day work of the Licence Working Group * a look at the future
