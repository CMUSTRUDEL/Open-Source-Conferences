	* Abstract:

Julius opens the conference and gives an overview of the state of Prometheus.

* Speaker biography:

Julius co-founded Prometheus and lead the project to success at SoundCloud and beyond. He now focuses on growing the Prometheus community and is the main organizer of PromCon 2016. In a previous life, Julius was a Site Reliability Engineer at Google.
