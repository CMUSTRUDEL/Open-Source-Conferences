Title: First PromCon North America and Prometheus Operator release! Prometheus Ecosystem Call - July 2021
Publication date: 2021-07-07
Playlist: Prometheus Community & The Ecosystem Meetings
Description: 
	PromCon North America 2021 is the seventh installment of the PromCon conference dedicated to the Prometheus monitoring system. The conference will be presented as a hybrid event - attendees can join and participate in person or virtually. It will take place on Monday, October 11, 2021, as a colocated event at KubeCon + CloudNativeCon North America.
Check it out at https://promcon.io/2021-losangeles/
