Title: Lessons from the Greenhouse: The Benefits and Challenges of Umbrella Open Source... Brian Behlendorf
Publication date: 2021-03-24
Playlist: ASWF Open Source Forum 2021 - Virtual
Description: 
	Lessons from the Greenhouse: The Benefits and Challenges of Umbrella Open Source Software Communities - Brian Behlendorf, Hyperledger

Many open source communities are actually communities of communities - what the LF called "Umbrella" organizations, though on Hyperledger we call it the "Greenhouse". This nesting arrangement has a lot of advantages, but can come with unique challenges as the number of projects and communities within the umbrella/greenhouse grows beyond a handful. Come to hear about the greenhouse-scaling issues Hyperledger has faced and had to overcome, and those that are still vexing.
