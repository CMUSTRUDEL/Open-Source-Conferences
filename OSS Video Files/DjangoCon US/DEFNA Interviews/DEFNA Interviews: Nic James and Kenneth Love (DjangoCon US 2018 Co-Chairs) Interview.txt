Title: DEFNA Interviews: Nic James and Kenneth Love (DjangoCon US 2018 Co-Chairs) Interview
Publication date: 2019-02-25

Description: 
	Today we are interviewing Nic James and Kenneth Love. They were Co-Chairs in the past DjangoCon US 2018.
Nic and Kenneth talk about how it was to become a co-chair of a conference of the size of DjangoCon US and give some good advice on how to start getting involved in these type of events.
