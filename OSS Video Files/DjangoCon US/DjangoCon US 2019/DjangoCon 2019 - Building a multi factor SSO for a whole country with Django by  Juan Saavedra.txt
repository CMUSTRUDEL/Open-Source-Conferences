Description: 
	DjangoCon 2019 - Building a multi factor SSO for a whole country with Django by  Juan Saavedra

As a leading digital country and a D9 member, Uruguay decided to build its own world class multi factor auth service for its citizens. We will show how Django once again delivered on its promises in a complex scenario, enabling a fast and flexible development of a secure and reliable solution.

This talk was presented at: https://2019.djangocon.us/talks/building-a-multi-factor-sso-for-a-whole/
