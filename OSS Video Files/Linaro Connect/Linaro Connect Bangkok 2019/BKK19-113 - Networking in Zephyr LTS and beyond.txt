Title: BKK19-113 - Networking in Zephyr LTS and beyond
Publication date: 2019-04-03
Playlist: Linaro Connect Bangkok 2019
Description: 
	Abstract
Zephyr 1.14 is a long-expected LTS (Long-Term Support) release for Zephyr RTOS (https://www.zephyrproject.org/), a Linux Foundation project . A lot of effort went both into extending networking support for it and making it stable. This session discusses both things which fit and didnt fit into the LTS release.

Paul Sokolovsky / IoT Engineer Linaro
Paul is a member of Linaro LITE team, who specializes in networking and application frameworks.
