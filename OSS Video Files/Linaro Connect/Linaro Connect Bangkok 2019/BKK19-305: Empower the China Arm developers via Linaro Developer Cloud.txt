Description: 
	Pengcheng Laboratory (PCL) is dedicated to the national strategy of Guangdong, Hong Kong and Macao District, with the goal of becoming a new national laboratory. Ecosystem development is one of the fundamentals of PCL. By working with worldwide industrial leaders, PCL is trying to drive the arm and linux technology to become Chinas independent and controllable pillars. Pengcheng Laboratory will work with Linaro to build a public-oriented developer cloud and software warehouse to jointly promote and improvement the latest arm linux ecosystem results.
