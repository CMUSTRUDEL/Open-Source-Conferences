Title: LVC21-300K1 Can Embedded and IoT Just Work Standards, SystemReady and Linux
Publication date: 2021-04-15
Playlist: Linaro Virtual Connect Spring 2021
Description: 
	This talk will provide an overview of Arm SystemReady, a recently launched certification program based on a set of standards that enable interoperability with generic, off-the-shelf operating systems and hypervisors. SystemReady extends the former Arm ServerReady program to the embedded and IoT sector and we will cover the steps needed to make software ‘Just Work’ on these diverse platforms.
