Title: LVC21-200K1 KEYNOTE Building the Future of Pervasive, Collaborative Intelligence with ARM and Open S
Publication date: 2021-04-15
Playlist: Linaro Virtual Connect Spring 2021
Description: 
	Technology is becoming more intelligent and available to us wherever we go. This will bring tremendous benefits for everything from entertainment to healthcare. But, as we work towards building this world of pervasive, collaborative intelligence, we must address several key challenges. Our clouds and data centers must evolve to support the demands of machine learning. Our devices must work together in new ways to inform and support our lives. And, we must protect our privacy, security, and values.
