Title: Keynote: Yes, You Could Help Build the Next Disruptor!! - Samaira Mehta
Publication date: 2019-09-12
Playlist: ApacheCon North America 2019
Description: 
	Samaira's captivating and interactive talk will focus on the power within each of us that would help to nurture the next disruptor. She opens the talk with how all of us have grown so accustomed to technology, like our phones, for our day to day life. But reminds us that technology is not the future, people are the future. Each of us has superpowers that can bring and develop change agents and disruptors. She will talk about her journey that made her CEO at the age of 7. Her journey is full of success and failure stories, all of which are basic ingredients of learning and bringing out best inside us. Come and be inspired by Samaira Mehta, the 11-year-old Entrepreneur.
