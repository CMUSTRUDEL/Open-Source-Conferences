Title: Need of Apache way in finTech Kanchana Welagedara
Publication date: 2019-06-19
Playlist: Apache Roadshow Chicago, 2019
Description: 
	The Apache Way is a set of community behaviors and aspects used to great success over many years at over 100 Apache Software Foundation projects. Fintech takes lessons learned from developing software at ASF and applies them to the way companies develop software internally.It builds up an great engineering echo system to deviate from traditional engineering silos and create more opportunities for innovation , business velocity and better engineering collaboration.
