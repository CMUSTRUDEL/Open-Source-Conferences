Title: 50 Shades of Bike Lane Obstructions Christina Whitehouse
Publication date: 2019-07-08
Playlist: Apache Roadshow Chicago, 2019
Description: 
	You name it, we've seen it. Each unique bike lane obstruction offers unique problems as well as opportunities to resolve them. While blocked bike lanes are a seemingly unsolvable problem, Bike Lane Uprising has accepted the challenge. Come learn how our guerrilla civic tech movement gained local and national headlines as well as government officials...but in a positive way.
