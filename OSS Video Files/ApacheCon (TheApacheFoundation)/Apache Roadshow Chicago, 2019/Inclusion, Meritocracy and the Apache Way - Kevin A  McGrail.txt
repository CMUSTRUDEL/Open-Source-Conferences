Title: Inclusion, Meritocracy and the Apache Way - Kevin A  McGrail
Publication date: 2019-06-07
Playlist: Apache Roadshow Chicago, 2019
Description: 
	This talk introduces attendees to some of the key points of the Apache Way especially focusing on meritocracy and how we encourage inclusion while simultaneously being elitist. Will cover the Apache Way Tenants: Charity (OSS at No Charge) Community (Community over Code) Consensus (Consensus versus Voting) Meritocracy (JFDI/BDFL) Transparency (If it didn't happen on list...) Pragmatic (Open Source Business Friendly)
