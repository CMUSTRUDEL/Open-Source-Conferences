Title: #ACEU19: Supporting Open Source In-House – An Experience with Cassandra
Publication date: 2019-10-31
Playlist: ApacheCon Europe 2019 – Berlin
Description: 
	Speakers: Jim Håkansson & Anuj Wadehra
More: https://aceu19.apachecon.com/session/supporting-open-source-house-experience-cassandra

In this presentation, Cassandra Architects at Ericsson shall share their vast experience in supporting 140+ Cassandra deployments in production. They will share the key challenges and best practices with respect to supporting an Open Source software such as Cassandra. Expect plenty of learning and interesting stuff when the team talks about many generic and Cassandra related problems which they faced in production and how they fixed each one of them successfully by providing unique solutions.

Audience: Organizations interested in building their own in-house capabilities to support open source software and all Cassandra users especially Cassandra operators and administrators.
YouTube URL: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=fNN6nyjUiNM
