Join us for the official presentation on the people, projects, and progress of The Apache Software Foundation (ASF), delivered by an ASF officer at every ApacheCon --2019 features ASF President Sam Ruby in Las Vegas and ASF Executive Vice President David Nalley in Berlin. Highlights include key activities and achievements by Apache projects and their communities, as well as ASF Operations, and a look back over the past year’s activities.
