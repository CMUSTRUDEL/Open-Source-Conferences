	In-break Artist Performance from RustFest Global 2020
Music: Dahlia Fae
Visuals: Jay Weeks

Dahlia Fae is an audio/video media artist. She produces music and visual art with themes of intense emotions, identity, spirituality, and of the queer experience. She spent the 2010s performing and contributing to the LGBT+ rave scene of New York City, USA, while creating an array of music and art of experimental genres. Dahlia currently lives in Los Angeles, USA, where she continues to create and perform.

https://dahliafae.bandcamp.com/
https://www.instagram.com/dahliafae.xo/
https://twitter.com/dahliafae_xo

Jay Weeks is a new-media artist and coder based in Los Angeles, USA. He enjoys working with themes of exploration, play, and discovery; primarily making interactive web-based media. The on-going project “Microbium” merges drawing with physically-based simulation, enabling new forms of organic sketching not wholly controlled by the user or the machine.
