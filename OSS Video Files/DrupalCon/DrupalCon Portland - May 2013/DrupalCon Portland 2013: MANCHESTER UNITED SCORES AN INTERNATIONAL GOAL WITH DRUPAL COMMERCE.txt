Title: DrupalCon Portland 2013: MANCHESTER UNITED SCORES AN INTERNATIONAL GOAL WITH DRUPAL COMMERCE
Publication date: 2013-05-28
Playlist: DrupalCon Portland - May 2013
Description:

Edoardo Abis, Head of Manchester United Schools at Nike
