Title: Streamline your development workflow with Lando and Lagoon   DrupalCon Europe 2020
Publication date: 2021-01-13
Playlist: DrupalCon Europe 2020
Description: 
	The founders of two developer-favorite open-source web dev tools, Lando and Lagoon, have joined forces to bring the best of local Drupal development to Kubernetes. In this talk, Mike and Michael will share the story behind the new, seamless Lando-to-Lagoon build and deploy solution and demonstrate how you can use it to improve your development workflow. They will also share their best strategies for faster, more frequent deployments, and nerd out together over the future of Drupal development. Ready for a more efficient workflow? Then this talk is for you.
