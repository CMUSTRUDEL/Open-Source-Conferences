Drupal founder Dries Buytaert takes the stage at DrupalCon Global for The Driesnote Plenary Session. Born in Belgium, Dries is regarded as pioneer in the open source web publishing and digital experience platform space. Always a highlight of the DrupalCon program, Dries will give an engaging talk about the state of the Drupal project as well as an overview of the recent Drupal 9 release and plans for Drupal 10.
