Title: Relaunch blog of Unity.com with Headless Drupal 8, Next.js   DrupalCon North America 2021
Publication date: 2021-05-27
Playlist: DrupalCon North America 2021
Description: 
	After many years of successfully running an interactive blog in 9 different languages using Wordpress, Unity decided to relaunch the blog using headless Drupal and Next.js. The goal was to build a state of the art blogging system that provides a state of the art editorial experience for the team and contributors of the blog and to decouple the frontend completely from the backend.
