Title: Drupal Association Webinar: Drupal 8 and Spark Simplify Responsive Design, Mobile
Publication date: 2013-04-26
Playlist: Drupal Association Webcasts
Description: 
	To build an effective digital experience, you must be able to easily create content on all devices without being slowed down by the need for extensive technical skills. The new Spark capabilities within Drupal 8, and separately downloadable for Drupal 7, allow content design and content updates to be made across Web and mobile channels with ease.

Presented by Kevin O'Leary, Spark UX Lead, and Jesse Beach, Senior Front End Engineer.
