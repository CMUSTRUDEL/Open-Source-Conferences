Title: Delivering the enterprise data cloud - Arun Murthy (Cloudera)
Publication date: 2019-09-26
Playlist: Strata Data Conference 2019 - New York, NY
Description: 
	An enterprise data cloud can unlock the value of any data anywhere at cloud speed and cloud scale. It has the power to transform our businesses and even the way we live. In this keynote, we’ll introduce you to the new 100% open source Cloudera Data Platform (CDP), the world’s first enterprise data cloud. CDP is hybrid and multi-cloud, delivering the speed, agility, and scale you need to secure and govern your data anywhere from the edge to AI.
