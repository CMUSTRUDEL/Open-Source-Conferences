Title: Driving enterprise open source adoption, from data lake to AI (sponsored by Teradata) Ron Bodkin
Publication date: 2017-03-16
Playlist: Strata + Hadoop World 2017 - San Jose, California
Description: 
	Ron Bodkin explains how Teradata drives open source adoption inside enterprises through scaling data management on Hadoop using open source projects like Kylo, streaming data with Listener, performing SQL on Hadoop with Presto, and modernizing analytics using AI techniques with Spark, Aster, and TensorFlow to address key enterprise requirements that integrate Hadoop to fully leverage the benefits of open source in their analytical ecosystems.

This keynote is sponsored by Teradata.
