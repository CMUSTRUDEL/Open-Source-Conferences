Title: Music and Jupyter: A Combo for Creating Collaborative Narratives
Publication date: 2017-11-08
Playlist: JupyterCon
Description: 
	Music engages and delights. Carol Willing (Cal Poly San Luis Obispo) explains how to explore and teach the basics of interactive computing and data science by combining music with Jupyter notebooks, using music21, a tool for computer-aided musicology, and Magenta, a TensorFlow project for making music with machine learning, to create collaborative narratives and publishing materials for teaching and learning.
