Title: Leveraging Jupyter to Build an Excel-Python Bridge
Publication date: 2017-11-08
Playlist: JupyterCon
Description: 
	Christine Doig (Anaconda ), Fabio Pliger (Anaconda) explain how they built a commercial product on top Jupyter to help Excel users access the capabilities of the rich data science Python ecosystem and share examples and use cases from a variety of industries that illustrate the collaborative workflow between analysts and data scientists that the application has enabled.
