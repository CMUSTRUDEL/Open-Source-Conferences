Title: Unifying analytics and AI on big data for faster insights at scale - Ziya Ma (Intel)
Publication date: 2019-06-29
Playlist: The O'Reilly Artificial Intelligence Conference 2019, Beijing, China
Description: 
	This is presented in Chinese. The business imperative for AI is firmly rooted in data. Data is the currency of the future, as it contains extremely valuable insights that will transform business, operations, security, and experiences across all industries. Harnessing greater and faster insights from data at scale requires a holistic and comprehensive data strategy that takes advantage of modern data-centric infrastructure advancements coupled with new open source software innovations that seamlessly unify advanced analytics with AI technologies.
