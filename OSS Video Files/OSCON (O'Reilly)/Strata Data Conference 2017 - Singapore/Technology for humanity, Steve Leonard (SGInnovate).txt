Title: Technology for humanity, Steve Leonard (SGInnovate)
Publication date: 2017-12-07
Playlist: Strata Data Conference 2017 - Singapore
Description: 
	Technology is the foundation for moving humanity forward. However, we sometimes get too focused on the technology itself—making it faster, better, smaller. How can we flip it around and focus on people? Steve Leonard details how Singapore is bringing together ambitious and capable individuals and teams to imagine, start, build, and scale technology that can solve the world’s toughest challenges.
