Title: Cultivating production excellence, Liz Fong-Jones (Honeycomb)
Publication date: 2019-06-13
Playlist: O'Reilly Velocity Conference 2019 - San Jose, CA
Description: 
	Taming the complex distributed systems we’re responsible for doesn’t just require changing the tools and technical approaches we use; it also requires changing who’s involved in production, how they collaborate, and how we measure success.

Join Liz Fong-Jones to explore several practices core to production excellence: giving everyone a stake in production, collaborating to ensure observability, measuring with service level objectives, and prioritizing improvements using risk analysis.
