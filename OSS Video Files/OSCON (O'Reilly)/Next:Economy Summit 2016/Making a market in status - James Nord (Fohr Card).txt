Title: Making a market in status - James Nord (Fohr Card)
Publication date: 2016-10-11
Playlist: Next:Economy Summit 2016
Description: 
	Science fiction has long posited a future of material abundance enabled by technology and a human economy built on creativity, reputation, and status. We may already be seeing signs of that science fiction economy. James Nord of Fohr Card shows us how the reputation gained through social media gets traded or converted into cold, hard cash.
