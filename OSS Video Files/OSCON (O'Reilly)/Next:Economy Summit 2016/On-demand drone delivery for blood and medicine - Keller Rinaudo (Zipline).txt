Title: On-demand drone delivery for blood and medicine - Keller Rinaudo (Zipline)
Publication date: 2016-10-11
Playlist: Next:Economy Summit 2016
Description: 
	While Amazon and Google are experimenting with drone delivery so rich consumers can get their latest purchases delivered to their door, Keller Rinaudo had a better idea. Let’s put on-demand technology and drones to work solving a real problem: delivering blood and critical medicines in settings where there is no widespread medical infrastructure and poor transportation. We’ll have a look at the technology Keller’s company Zipline is deploying today in Rwanda.
