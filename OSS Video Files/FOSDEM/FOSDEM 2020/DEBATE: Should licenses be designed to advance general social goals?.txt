Title: DEBATE: Should licenses be designed to advance general social goals?
Publication date: 2020-07-13
Playlist: FOSDEM 2020
Description: 
	by John Sullivan, Molly de Blanc, James Vasile, Josh Simmons and Dashiell Renaud

At: FOSDEM 2020
https://video.fosdem.org/2020/UA2.220/debate_socal_goal_licenses.webm

We have seen several licenses proposed as "open source" that
carry some obligation or restriction related to ethics or
other social goals. Is this a good direction for FOSS license drafting?
Affirmative position: FOSS licenses should advance social goals
