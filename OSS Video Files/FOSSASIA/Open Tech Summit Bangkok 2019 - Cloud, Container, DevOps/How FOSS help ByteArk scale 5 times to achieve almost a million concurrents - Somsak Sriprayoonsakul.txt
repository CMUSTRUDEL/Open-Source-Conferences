Title: How FOSS help ByteArk scale 5 times to achieve almost a million concurrents - Somsak Sriprayoonsakul
Publication date: 2019-10-20
Playlist: Open Tech Summit Bangkok 2019 - Cloud, Container, DevOps
Description: 
	Somsak Sriprayoonsakul

ByteArk is a CDN platform providing services video streaming &amp; web-site. The platform has passed a certain milestone that the platform could withstand almost a million concurrent viewers in a single event by scaling the platform 5 times of its former capacity in around 2 months. This presentation will share the details on how open-source software help achieve this milestone.
