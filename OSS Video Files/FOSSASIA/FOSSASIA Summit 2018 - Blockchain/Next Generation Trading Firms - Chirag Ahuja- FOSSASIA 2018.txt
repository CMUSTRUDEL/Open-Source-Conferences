Among all animals only humans show the curiosity for swapping and exchanging goods. This gave birth to the idea of trade which enabled large spread collaboration and pole vaulted us to spread innovation goods and services globally. 

In this talk let's explore how energy and commodity trading firms are viewing technologies such as blockchain smart contracts robotic process automation cloud computing open source &amp artificial intelligence to build the next generation of trading organizations. We'll look at the impact it would have on the skill sets required by future traders and how "brain-power" (intuition) and "computing-power" (mechanics) could work together in seamless harmony  orchestrated together perfectly like Beethoven's 9th Symphony.
