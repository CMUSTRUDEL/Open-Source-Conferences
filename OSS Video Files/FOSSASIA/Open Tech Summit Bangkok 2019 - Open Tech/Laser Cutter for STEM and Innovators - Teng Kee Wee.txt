Title: Laser Cutter for STEM and Innovators - Teng Kee Wee
Publication date: 2019-10-19

Description: 
	Teng Kee Wee, CEO Lionsforge (Singapore)

How can a Lasercutter benefit students in their STEM education and get them ready to be innovators and entrepreneurs? This is one goal we aim for with the Lasercraft cutter of Lionsforge. Designed from the the ground up to be reliable, user friendly and maintenance free the CraftLaser is a valuable ally to any crafter and a new source of revenue for entrepreneurs. How you use it and what you create with it is only limited by the imagination.
