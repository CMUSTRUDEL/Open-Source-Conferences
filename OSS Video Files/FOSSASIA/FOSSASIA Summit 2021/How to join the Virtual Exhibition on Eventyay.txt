The same flow applies for other conference hosted on Eventyay http://eventyay.com​​ Eventyay is an #opensource​​ event manage system developed by #FOSSASIA​​. It comes with a wide range of features such as video integration, call for speakers, scheduling, virtual exhibition, calendar, ticketing and more.
