Title: Open@RIT, Our Model for an Academic OSPO (Stephen Jacobs)
Publication date: 2021-03-08
Playlist: FOSSASIA Summit 2021
Description: 
	About the speaker: Stephen Jacobs is the Director of Open@RIT, the new Open Programs Office at the Rochester Institute of Technology and a professor with their School of Interactive Games and Media. Jacobs got involved in Open Source when he decided to create a class centered around educational game development for the One Laptop per Child. This initial work led to the creation of the first academic minor in Free and Open Source Software and Free Culture and most recently Open@RIT.
