Title: Diversity and Inclusion in Tech Panel, FOSSASIA Summit 2021
Publication date: 2021-04-13
Playlist: FOSSASIA Summit 2021
Description: 
	Host: Sherie Ng, General Manager for Public Sector Asia Pacific, Microsoft

Speakers: Bich Hanh Duong (Chief Culture Unit, UNESCO Bangkok), Hong Phuc Dang (Founder, FOSSASIA) and Kiruthika Ramanathan (Deputy Director, Education Program, Science Centre Singapore)

This panel discussion highlights the importance of diversity and inclusion in the field of technology with unique stories of leaders and experts from different backgrounds. Panelists will discuss their problem-solving strategies, their work environments, their teams, and the different ways they are using open technology to impact change. Panelists will also share details about their work-life, what it takes to become successful in the rapidly changing field of technology, the importance of diversity and inclusion in their field.
