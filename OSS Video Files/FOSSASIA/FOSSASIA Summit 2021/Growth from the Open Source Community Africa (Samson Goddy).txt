Title: Growth from the Open Source Community Africa (Samson Goddy)
Publication date: 2021-03-14
Playlist: FOSSASIA Summit 2021
Description: 
	About the speaker: Samson Goddy is a software developer who believes in changing the world, an open source advocate, primarily maintaining Sugar Desktop while also serving on the board of directors with Sugar Labs, and sustaining Open Source Collective as a board member. He also co-founded Open Source Community Africa, a movement that promotes and educates everything open source within Africa. He is currently doing open source with Ovio as a program manager.
