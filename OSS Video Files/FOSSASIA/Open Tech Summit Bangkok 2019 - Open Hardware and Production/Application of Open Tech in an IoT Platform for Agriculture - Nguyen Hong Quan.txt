Title: Application of Open Tech in an IoT Platform for Agriculture - Nguyen Hong Quan
Publication date: 2019-10-20
Playlist: Open Tech Summit Bangkok 2019 - Open Hardware and Production
Description: 
	Nguyen Hong Quan

The major advantage of an IoT platform for farming is the integration into a one platform to control farming decisions such as irrigation system control, fertilization, pesticides, and an open data network. How we apply open tech from various levels (operating system, embedded system, database, programming language, web framework) to build up an IoT platform for mushroom farm, which then expand to hydroponics and swiftlethouse.
