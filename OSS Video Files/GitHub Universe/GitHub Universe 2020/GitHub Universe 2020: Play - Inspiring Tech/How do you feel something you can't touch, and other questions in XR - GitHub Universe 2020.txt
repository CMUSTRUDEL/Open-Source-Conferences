Description: 
	Presented by Eliza Struthers-Jobin, artist & programmer, Independent

While imagining the hidden spaces that exist in the overlap between the physical and the digital, Eliza discovered a place neither quite here or there where she has set up shop. Using devices and digital intelligence as her tools, she explores our sense of play, and challenges our sense of presence and perception to bring us back to ourselves.
