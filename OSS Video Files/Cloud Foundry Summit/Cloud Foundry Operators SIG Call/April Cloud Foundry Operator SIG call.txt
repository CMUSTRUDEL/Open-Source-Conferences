Title: April Cloud Foundry Operator SIG call
Publication date: 2019-04-26
Playlist: Cloud Foundry Operators SIG Call
Description: 
	The Cloud Foundry Operators Special Interest Group will bring those within the Cloud Foundry community who are highly interested in the operator's landscape together for a monthly discussion. The goal is to discuss and exchange ideas and best practices to enable de facto standards within the community and increase operator efficiency.
