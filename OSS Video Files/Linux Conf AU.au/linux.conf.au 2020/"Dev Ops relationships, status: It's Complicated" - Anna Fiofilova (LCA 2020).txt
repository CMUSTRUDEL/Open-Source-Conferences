This is short and entertaining talk in stand up comedy style about evolution of a difficult relationships between Developers and Ops. 
The story will be told from a Developer perspective of one person.  
You will remember good old days, learn a few DevOps tips and tricks and hopefully have some empathy for your colleagues on the other side of DevOps :) 

No prior knowledge of any tech is required. Bring on your sense of humour!
