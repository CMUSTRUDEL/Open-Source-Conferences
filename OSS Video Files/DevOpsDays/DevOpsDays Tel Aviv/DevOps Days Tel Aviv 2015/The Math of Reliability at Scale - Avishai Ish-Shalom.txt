Title: The Math of Reliability at Scale - Avishai Ish-Shalom
Publication date: 2015-11-01
Playlist: DevOps Days Tel Aviv 2015
Description: 
	http://www.devopsdays.org/events/2015-telaviv/

We often hear talks of "scale" and "reliability", mostly based on personal experience and lessons learned. What can Mathematics tell us about reliability and scale? Can math help us scale our systems and companies? It turns out that failure models, probability, statistics and other domains can help our analysis and provide insights.

About the speaker - Avishai Ish-Shalom

Avishai is a veteran Ops and a survivor of many production skirmishes. Currently masquerading as the CTO of Fewbytes, Avishai help companies deal with web era operations and scale. In his spare time Avishai is spreading weird ideas and conspiracy theories such as DevOps.
