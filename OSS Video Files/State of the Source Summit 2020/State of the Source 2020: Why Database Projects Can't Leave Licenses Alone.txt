Description: 
	Why Database Projects Can't Leave Licenses Alone
Josh Berkus

You may have heard the controversy around new licenses like the SSPL, Commons Clause, and CAL. It's not a coincidence that these come from database software; in fact, it's a tradition. For the last 25 years, database projects and vendors have been messing around with licenses. Learn about the history of database licensing and enforcement, including MySQL, Ingres, PostgreSQL, MongoDB, Redis, and more. We'll delve into the reasons why they change licenses, and what some alternatives are.
