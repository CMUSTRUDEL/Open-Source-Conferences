Title: OSMC 2017 | With a little help from…the community by Alba Ferri
Publication date: 2017-12-04
Playlist: OSMC 2017 | Open Source Monitoring Conference
Description: 
	When we think in Open Source, we can’t forget the fundamental essence that sustains it, this is the Community. From my point of view, Icinga2 has shown to have a great and generous community, that has helped me to customize the monitoring platform of Vodafone Spain, in a fantastic and optimal way. I would like to share with you the different approaches we have choose to monitor such a heterogeneous environment.
