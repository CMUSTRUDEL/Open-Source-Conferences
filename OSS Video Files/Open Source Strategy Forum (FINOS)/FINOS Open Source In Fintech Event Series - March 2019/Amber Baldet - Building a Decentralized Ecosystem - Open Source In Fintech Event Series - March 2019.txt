From the FINOS Open Source In Fintech Event Series - March 5th 2019

Decentralized technologies have the potential to fundamentally change the landscape of the financial services industry. By providing a level playing field for everyone - from large firms to individual developers - we can accelerate delivery of real-life distributed applications that provide tangible value and solve real world problems.

Amber Baldet, CEO of Clovyr discusses how decentralized technology collaboration is already transforming the industry. She also talks about how the FINOS community, through its Decentralized program, is providing a venue for future collaboration.
