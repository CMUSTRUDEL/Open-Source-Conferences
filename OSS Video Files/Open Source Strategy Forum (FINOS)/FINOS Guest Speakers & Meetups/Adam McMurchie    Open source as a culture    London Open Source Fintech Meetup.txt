Title: Adam McMurchie    Open source as a culture    London Open Source Fintech Meetup
Publication date: 2021-01-08
Playlist: FINOS Guest Speakers & Meetups
Description: 
	ADAM MCMURCHIE // HEAD OF DEVOPS, CHIEF SECURITY OFFICE // BARCLAYS
In his talk, Adam shares some of his remarkable personal journey to illustrate the importance of fostering an open source culture – it is *this* culture that is driving innovations in fintech and artificial intelligence, and bringing the new talent and ideas into the financial services sector that will shape its future.

The London Open Source Fintech Meetup is organised in partnership by Scott Logic (https://www.scottlogic.com) and FINOS (https://www.finos.org).
