	Speaker: Amber Baldet
Title: Coordinate, Collaborate, Compete: Open Tools for the Next-Generation Financial Industry

Amber led J.P. Morgan’s Blockchain Center of Excellence since its inception in 2015, recently leaving to launch Clovyr. She has sought to increase engagement in blockchain development within the information security community, leading integration of some of the first advanced bug hunting tools into the open source Quorum blockchain platform, as well as presenting at Empire Hacking, Hack in the Box, and elsewhere. Appearing on 2017’s Fortune’s 40 Under 40 list of the most influential young people in business and CoinDesk’s 10 Most Influential in Blockchain, she “handily bridges the divide between the Wall Street and crypto sets.
