Title: Angular with Ivy: A sneak-peek into the future of Angular - Andrei Antal | JSHeroes Meetups
Publication date: 2020-04-10
Playlist: JSHeroes Meetups 2020
Description: 
	Angular 9 just launched and its most exciting feature is Ivy, the new rendering engine. In this presentation, we'll have a look at the core principles of Ivy, how it compares to its predecessors and how it will enable smaller and faster Angular applications. We'll also talk about some of the new capabilities it will enable in the future like module-less components, lazy-loaded components, higher-order components and many more.

Bio:
Andrei is a frontend developer and an Angular consultant. On the side, he's a trainer for JSLeague where he teaches javascript and Angular development in both enterprise and community workshops. In his free time, he organizes the Bucharest Angular community - ngBucharest.

Required knowledge:
A basic understanding of the Angular framework will make some of the topics in the presentation easier to understand and relate to, but otherwise, you should be able to follow along with the content.
