Description: 
	Keynote: Open Source and Startup Business Models - Priyanka Sharma, Director of Technical Evangelism, GitLab and Governing Board Member, CNCF

Commercial open source software companies  (COSS) are a net positive for the open source community but face myriad challenges building a business. In the age of hyper clouds, building application software and monetizing it with the buyer-based open core business model has been successful for GitLab. This talk will share the details.
