	Keynote: Our Responsibility to the Global Open Source Community - Erica Brescia, Chief Operating Officer, GitHub

As we all know, open source now powers much of the world and touches every aspect of our lives. Today's evolving geopolitical landscape is causing questions about the potential fragmentation of the open source community along national borders. We, as a community, cannot let this happen. I'll share a few ideas of what we can do to prevent this.
