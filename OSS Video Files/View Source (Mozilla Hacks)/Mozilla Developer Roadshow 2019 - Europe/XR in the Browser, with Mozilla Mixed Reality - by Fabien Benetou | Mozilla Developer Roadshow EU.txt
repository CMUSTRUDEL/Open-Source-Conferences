Title: XR in the Browser, with Mozilla Mixed Reality - by Fabien Benetou | Mozilla Developer Roadshow EU
Publication date: 2019-10-14
Playlist: Mozilla Developer Roadshow 2019 - Europe
Description: 
	An introduction to the emerging fields of virtual reality and augmented reality, where use cases often feel like sci-fi concepts brought to life. In this talk, Fabien Benetou explores the primitives that are the building blocks of every VR and AR experience and the Mozilla tools you can use to push the boundaries of reality in these exciting new technologies. 

Fabien Benetou is a WebXR developer consulting for the European Parliament, the UNICEF Innovation Fund and more.
