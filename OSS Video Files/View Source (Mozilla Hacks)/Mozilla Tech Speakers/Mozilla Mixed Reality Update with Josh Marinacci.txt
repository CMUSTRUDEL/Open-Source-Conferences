Title: Mozilla Mixed Reality Update with Josh Marinacci
Publication date: 2018-07-20
Playlist: Mozilla Tech Speakers
Description: 
	In this Tech Briefing, Mozilla Mixed Reality advocate Josh Marinacci brings us up to date on recent developments from the VR, AR, and XR frontlines.

Josh covers everything from the latest headsets to the state of WebXR specs, including updates on new projects from Mozilla like the Firefox Reality browser, and Mozilla Hubs, a virtual reality space where you can get together online.
