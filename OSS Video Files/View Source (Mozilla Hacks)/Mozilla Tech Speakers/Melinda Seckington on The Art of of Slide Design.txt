Title: Melinda Seckington on The Art of of Slide Design
Publication date: 2017-11-25
Playlist: Mozilla Tech Speakers
Description: 
	Melinda Seckington looks at how you can improve your presentations with some simple tips and tricks to make your slide designs better. This talk examines how you can use fonts, colours, animations and more to support the story you want to tell.
