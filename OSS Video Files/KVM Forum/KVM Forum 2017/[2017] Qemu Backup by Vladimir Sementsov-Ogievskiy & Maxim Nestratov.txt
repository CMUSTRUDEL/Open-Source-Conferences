Title: [2017] Qemu Backup by Vladimir Sementsov-Ogievskiy & Maxim Nestratov
Publication date: 2017-11-08
Playlist: KVM Forum 2017
Description: 
	Creating backups is one of important tasks about virtual machines. In this presentation, Vladimir will review current state of drive backup in Qemu, new features and improvements develped in Virtuozzo (and not yet merged into upstream), future plans. The following topics will be touched: incremental backups, backup performance, external backups, Interferring with a guest.

---

Maxim Nestratov
Virtuozzo
Lead Software Developer

Maxim has a deep networking and virtualization background as he worked as a lead developer and a maintainer of networking core in Virtuozzo Containers for Windows for more than 3 years. He has been working also on Parallels Desktop for Mac project as a snapshot & suspend & resume features owner for more than 5 years.
Recently he has switched his focus on OpenStack integration with Virtuozzo maintaining Virtuozzo driver in libvirt.

Vladimir Sementsov-Ogievskiy
Virtuozzo
software developer

Vladimir Sementsov-Ogievskiy is a postgraduate student of Moscow Institute of Physics and Technology. He have received a master's degree at Applied Mathematics and Physics in 2014 in the same institute, with scientific work about virtual machine testing. Now he deals with virtual machine backup for Qemu. Vladimir works in Virtuozzo as a software developer. Vladimir lives in Moscow, Russia.
