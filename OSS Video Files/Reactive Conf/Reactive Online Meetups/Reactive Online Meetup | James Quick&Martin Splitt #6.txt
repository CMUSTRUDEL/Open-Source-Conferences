I'm Stuck and I Don't Know What To Do

𝗧𝗔𝗟𝗞 𝗔𝗕𝗦𝗧𝗥𝗔𝗖𝗧
Feel stuck in your job or career? Trust me, we've all been there. Believe it or not, though, you alone have the ability to influence and control the path that your career takes. Although it may seem overwhelming, it just takes a strategy to get started in the right direction. In this talk, we'll discuss how to achieve happiness in your career whatever that may look like for you.

𝗕𝗜𝗢
James is a developer, speaker, and teacher with a passion for web development, design, and developer tools. He is a Senior Developer Advocate at Auth0 and has over 7 years of experience in Advocacy and Software Development. In his spare time, he runs a YouTube channel, plays co-ed soccer with his wife, spends time with his dogs, and can solve a Rubik's cube in under two minutes.

Technical SEO for modern web apps

𝗧𝗔𝗟𝗞 𝗔𝗕𝗦𝗧𝗥𝗔𝗖𝗧
When we build web apps, we want people to use them. One of the biggest sources for new users is search engines. But what about web apps built with modern web frameworks? Do we need SEO? What can we developers do to make our web apps findable and what goes into technical SEO? Let's walk through an example!

𝗕𝗜𝗢
