Description
Is an observable executed synchronously?
You might think you know it, most ppl don’t.

Schedulers is a topic which is not documented at all.
In this talk I will introduce you into the world of schedulers, it’s secrets and advantages. I will show you how to use them in basic cases, as well as advanced scenarios.

Outcome

After this session you will:

master smooth animations
solving complex timing errors
controlling execution context like an expert

And all this with schedulers.
