Title: FSOSS 2016: Learning Without Written Text
Publication date: 2016-11-25
Playlist: FSOSS 2016
Description: 
	Speaker: Tom Marazzo, Founder with GlyphicStudyNotes.com

If a picture says a thousand words - lets start making pictures! "Glyphic Study Notes" is a web app for students that is used for converting text into graphic images. The goal is for students to learn specific subjects and to relate major concepts to other concepts - graphically.
