Title: FSOSS 2016: The Datacenter In Your Car and the Encryption Engine In Your Smoke Detector
Publication date: 2016-11-24
Playlist: FSOSS 2016
Description: 
	Speaker: Chris Tyler, Industrial Research Chair with Seneca CDOT

High availability, advanced diagnostics, online backup, deep encryption, and redundant storage used to be features of mission-critical enterprise computing that were not required in little embedded systems. But embedded systems that are quite literally guarding our lives -- and soon, driving us around -- are clearly "mission-critical". How do we ensure that these systems are sufficiently safe, and what role does open source play in our future where computing is pervasive?
