Title: FSOSS 2016: Meteor, React, and Cordova Through the Ages
Publication date: 2016-11-24
Playlist: FSOSS 2016
Description: 
	Speakers: Oleksandr Zhurbenko, Research Assistant with Seneca CDOT and Maxim Khlobystov, Software Developer with Mate1.com
