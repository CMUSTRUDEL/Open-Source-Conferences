Title: Anil A. and Balaji S. @ Spinnaker.Live: Zero to 1000 Applications  - Spinnaker at Cisco
Publication date: 2020-06-20
Playlist: Spinnaker.Live 2020
Description: 
	Cisco adopted Spinnaker to modernize its continuous delivery process as part of its Cloud Native transformation. In this talk, we will explore what were some of the motivations to consider Spinnaker and the challenges that Cisco was trying to solve. We will then explore how they evaluated Spinnaker and onboarded applications and users. Along the way, learn about how they overcame challenges and achieved 1000+ applications milestone on their Spinnaker platform. Hear about the lessons learned and best practices implemented as well as the CD transformation accomplishments.
