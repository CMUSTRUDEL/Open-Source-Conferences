Title: Open Source Friday stream with Jofferson Ramirez Tiquez from Vue Stripe Checkout
Publication date: 2020-11-03
Playlist: Open Source Friday
Description: 
	This is our VOD (recording) from the Open Source Friday live stream on the 30th October 2020, 2pm AEDT. We interviewed Jofferson Ramirez Tiquez from Vue Stripe Checkout, a Vue JS Plugin for Stripe. We had the chance to chat about Open Source; what it is, how to get involved, what the community is like. Joff gave us some top tips on ensuring the success of your project, mobile apps, learning Vue, and lots of info about Documentation.
