Title: Regulating the Internet’s Future - The State of Technology Policy Making in India
Publication date: 2020-05-12
Playlist: Emerging Technologies Speaker Series
Description: 
	Debates over internet policy in India are raging right now. Just in the last year, India has seen major debates on data protection, encryption, intermediary liability, disinformation, data localization, and the world’s largest biometric identity database (the Aadhaar). Nikhil Pahwa, the co-founder of Medianama, TED Fellow, and an “Indian of Tomorrow” will deliver a talk on  “Regulating the Internet’s Future: The State of Technology Policy Making in India”.
