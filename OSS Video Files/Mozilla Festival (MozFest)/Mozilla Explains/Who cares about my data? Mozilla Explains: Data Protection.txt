Title: Who cares about my data? Mozilla Explains: Data Protection
Publication date: 2021-07-06
Playlist: Mozilla Explains
Description: 
	Why are companies and governments collecting data about you? How are they keeping it safe? And why should you care? 

In this episode of Mozilla Explains, Alice Munyua, director of Mozilla's Africa Innovation Program, tells us why protection of personal information is fundamental to any open and democratic society.
