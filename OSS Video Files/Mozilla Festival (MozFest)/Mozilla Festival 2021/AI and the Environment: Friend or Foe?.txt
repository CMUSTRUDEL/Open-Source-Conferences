Title: AI and the Environment: Friend or Foe?
Publication date: 2021-03-11
Playlist: Mozilla Festival 2021
Description: 
	This panel discussion plans to bring together leading journalists and experts who look at AI and the environment: both the unharnessed potential and potential pitfalls. We know that running AI -- including storing the data that enables it -- takes a huge toll on the environment. The question at hand is: is it worth it? How can AI be harnessed for climate modeling, satellite imagery and weather patterns to help farmers etc.? And, how do we balance the risks? Join us to find out. Panelists: Khari Johnson, Jackie Snow and Arunesh Sinha Moderator: Fieke Jansen
