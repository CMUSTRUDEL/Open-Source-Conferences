Title: Alternative Business Models For The Web
Publication date: 2021-03-25
Playlist: Mozilla Festival 2021
Description: 
	Join Ayman Hariri (CEO & Founder, Vero), Tony Fadell (founder, Nest & Principal at Future Shape) and Ashley Boyd (VP of Advocacy and Engagement, Mozilla Foundation) for a fascinating discussion on creating alternative business models for the web. Building on their experience, they'll provide insights on how you transform an idea into a sustainable business and explore the need to move away from the "free" ad-based economic model dominating the web today.
