Title: Privacy & Security Creepathon: The Results
Publication date: 2021-03-25
Playlist: Mozilla Festival 2021
Description: 
	What “creepy” personal information is available in public datasets? In our last creepathon, students explored a public dataset and you'll be shocked what was found. Learn from the winning students at CODAM, coding college in Amsterdam, as they present revealing stories found within this Venmo dataset and recommendations or policies they would implement to protect user privacy.
