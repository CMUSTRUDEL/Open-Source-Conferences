Title: RealTime Exoskeleton-Mapping and Body Segmentation on the Browser with Tensorflow.js - Italo Jose
Publication date: 2020-10-03
Playlist: CityJS Conf 2020
Description: 
	Are you already through in mapping your exoskeleton like Kinect and at the same time segment your body separable like your head, foot, hand and etc just by webcam? So, in this talk, we will show how we can implement a deep learning model with Tensorflow.js that makes it for us, and the best part, everything running in the browser!

ITALO JOSE GONCALVES DE OLIVEIRA

Italo José is Microsoft MVP in AI category and works at Vortx as a lead solutions architect, also he's Lead of Developer Circle SP from Facebook in São Paulo and is a Self-driving car assistant teacher at FIAP university in the mechatronic course. He wins (3º) the real self-driving car global hackathon in China by Pix Moving in 2019 and contributes with the community speaking about AI, mentoring communities by the communup.tech project and writing on medium and in the IMaster (IT Forum) as a columnist.
