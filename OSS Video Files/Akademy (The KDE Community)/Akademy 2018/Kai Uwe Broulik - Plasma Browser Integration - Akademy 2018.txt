Title: Kai Uwe Broulik - Plasma Browser Integration - Akademy 2018
Publication date: 2018-09-06
Playlist: Akademy 2018
Description: 
	With a significant portion of the user's time spent in applications outside the realm of Plasma, namely most web browsers, there's a need for better integration of those applications into the shell. Plasma Browser Integration provides media controls for websites, download status, KDE Connect integration, and more.
