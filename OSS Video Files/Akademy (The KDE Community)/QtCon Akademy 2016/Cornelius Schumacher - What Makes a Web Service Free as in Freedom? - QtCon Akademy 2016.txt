Title: Cornelius Schumacher - What Makes a Web Service Free as in Freedom? - QtCon Akademy 2016
Publication date: 2016-09-04
Playlist: QtCon Akademy 2016
Description: 
	KDE is all about free software. If you run this yourself you enjoy its freedoms, you are in control of your digital life. The situations is different when using software which is run by others as a service. How can we make sure that this is respecting your freedoms a well and that you keep the control over your digital life. This presentation is intended to be the beginning of an answer to this question.
