Title: Keynote Speaker: Lars Knoll - Towards Qt 6 - Akademy 2019
Publication date: 2020-05-25
Playlist: Akademy 2019
Description: 
	Qt has been the basis for KDE since its inception more than 20 years ago. After 7 years with Qt 5, work has now started towards a new major version of the frameworks. The talk will go through the main ideas and goals for Qt 6, focusing on high level ideas, but also going into some concrete details.
