Title: Dan Leinir Turthra Jensen - Software Distribution: Discussion + Lightning Talks - Akademy 2019
Publication date: 2020-05-25
Playlist: Akademy 2019
Description: 
	Getting software to end users has always been an issue those who create the software have been worried about. A great many solutions have been proposed and attempted for this problem. In this panel the different approaches will be discussion and questions from the audience answered.

In the lightning talk round you will hear about the different approaches from the people involved.
