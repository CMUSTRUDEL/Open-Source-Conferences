Title: Akademy 2020 - Carl Schwan - How to Create a Good Promotional  Website for your Project
Publication date: 2020-11-27
Playlist: Akademy 2020
Description: 
	Having a good website for your project should be a fundamental part of your application promotion. This is not an easy task, and in this task I will explain how to make it easier for you to get it right.

I will present the KDE Jekyll theme, the motivation behind this project, and briefly explain how it can be used to create a KDE website.

The second part will show some bad examples of websites and how they can be improved to make your project attractive to a potential new user.
