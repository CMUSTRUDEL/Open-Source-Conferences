Title: Akademy 2020 - Bhushan Shah - Virtual Sprints
Publication date: 2020-11-27
Playlist: Akademy 2020
Description: 
	After COVID-19, it is becoming increasingly hard to do a in-person sprint. But is that a bad thing? What are alternatives? Let's find out.

Bhushan has organized two virtual sprints so far,

- Plasma Mobile mini sprint
- Plasma Virtual sprint

Both sprints were using the BigBlueButton instance. This talk gives insights on how to successfully organize virtual sprint, how to deal with the time-zones, and overall organization of Virtual Sprint.

Bhushan will also share the benefits of the Virtual Sprint for attendees.
