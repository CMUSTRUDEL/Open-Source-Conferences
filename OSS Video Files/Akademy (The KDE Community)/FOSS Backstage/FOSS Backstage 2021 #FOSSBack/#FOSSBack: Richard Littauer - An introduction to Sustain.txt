Title: #FOSSBack: Richard Littauer - An introduction to Sustain
Publication date: 2021-03-02
Playlist: FOSS Backstage 2021 #FOSSBack
Description: 
	Sustain is a community which holds a space for conversations concerning open source sustainability. We have working groups that cover licensing, ethics, corporate accountability, design, environmental sustainability, marketing, and more. I'll give a brief overview of current activity at Sustain in this talk, including our maintainer fund we're launching with Gitcoin.
