Title: #FOSSBack: Eileen Wagner - Design FAQs from 5 Years of Coaching FOSS Projects
Publication date: 2021-03-01
Playlist: FOSS Backstage 2021 #FOSSBack
Description: 
	As designers supporting the Prototype Fund, we coached over 100 small and medium-sized teams since 2016. This talk goes over the most asked questions we get in these coachings, and points to our common answers and additional resources.
