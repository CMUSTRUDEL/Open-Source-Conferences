This talk will try to give an idea on the current state of Debian Community in India.
briefly it will cover the following topics
- Current state of Debian in India
- Geographical presence of Community members in India
- Diversity of people in the community
- Contribution to Debian by Indian Debian Community
- Achievements
- Problems with the current state, and probable ways to resolve them.
etc.
