	by Sahil Dhiman

At: MiniDebConf India 2021

The talk will serve as an introduction to Debian ecosystem and the community that makes it possible.

How a collection of more than  60000 packages gets updated and released in the biggest GNU/Linux distribution on earth. Who are the people behind it, what they do, why we do it and how to join to contribute in various ways possible. And ofcourse the Debian India community and events would be introduced. 

This talk is for folks new to Debian and want to learn more about it while also giving back to the community.
