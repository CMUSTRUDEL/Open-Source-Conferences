Title: OW2con19: Open Source UX: Duty to Rescue - Nicolas Vérité
Publication date: 2019-06-20

Description: 
	We are going to focus on user experiences of instant messaging throughout different technological revolutions of the last two decades. The result is quite mediocre, and we are all aware of it, although the ground is highly favorable for innovation and the big bazaar of user testing. Let's find out what's not working, and propose some solutions.
