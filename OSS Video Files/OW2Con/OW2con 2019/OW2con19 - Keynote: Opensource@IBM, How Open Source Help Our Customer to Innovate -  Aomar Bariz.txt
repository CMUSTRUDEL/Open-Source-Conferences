Title: OW2con19 - Keynote: Opensource@IBM, How Open Source Help Our Customer to Innovate -  Aomar Bariz
Publication date: 2019-06-19
Playlist: OW2con 2019
Description: 
	Information Governance Technical Leader at IBM France, Aomar Bariz confirms the rapid growth of Kubernetes in today's IT projects, sometimes associated to the Tekton framework in CI/CD chains. However, organisations have to integrate multiple Open Source Software including blockchain, AI and infrastructure components. Packaging enterprise-class applications becomes a key challenge, in a context where software licences are evolving.
